Hello, my name is Ben Russell.

I'm a project manager-turned-designer, and I'm passionate about the details. Keep reading for more info on me and my projects. I'm currently open to opportunities.

My philosophy

There is beauty in simplicity. I've seen some great ideas fail because a smart person tried to do too much. Whatever your goal or business is, stick to what you do best, and stop there. As Ron Swanson wisely said, "Never half-ass two things. Whole-ass one thing."

The right tool for the job

After experimenting with FluidUI, Invision, and *gasp* pen and paper, Figma is my weapon of choice when it comes to design. I enjoy discovering new ways to automate the boring stuff, including components, variants, and Auto Layout.

A little more about me

Besides project management and design, I also have experience in frontend development, including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Angular Material, and vanilla JavaScript. On the personal side, I've lived in Texas for fifteen years. I like writing, music, and writing about music.
